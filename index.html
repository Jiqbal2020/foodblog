Why we are unique

Taj India lets you enjoy authentic Far Eastern dishes. Many of our regular customers come back to taste our speciality – the unique bread baked in the original Tandoor oven. This is a forerunner of the modern,well known barbecue, where over the bowl in the form of inverted bell, the specialties are being baked by hot air. This Indian specialty called Nann – a large bread in the shape of a big drop – leaves us with an irreplaceable impression of crossing the Kashmir gate.
